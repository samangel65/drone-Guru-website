About Drone Guru

Pioneering the Future of Aerial Technology

Founded in Kathmandu, Drone Guru Pvt. Ltd. was born from a passion for flight and a vision for the future. We believe that drones are not just gadgets, but powerful tools that can revolutionize industries, capture breathtaking perspectives, and create new opportunities. Our mission is to provide world-class drone education and services, making aerial technology accessible, safe, and effective for everyone. Our team consists of government-certified pilots, expert technicians, and creative professionals dedicated to excellence in every flight and every project.

Our Services

Drone Pilot Training

From beginner fundamentals to advanced certification courses, we provide hands-on training to make you a confident and certified drone pilot.

Aerial Photography & Video

Industrial Inspection
