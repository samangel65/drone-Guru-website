Meet Suresh Shrestha

Our Chief Drone Pilot at Drone Guru

Chief Drone Pilot

Suresh Shrestha is the Chief Drone Pilot at Drone Guru, bringing over five years of hands-on experience in the UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) industry. A highly qualified and skilled drone professional, Suresh holds a Bachelor's degree in Aviation from Griffith University and is licensed by CASA (Civil Aviation Safety Authority) to operate UAVs.

Licenses & Certifications:

  • ARN: 1204056
  • Commercial Drone License: CASA Certified
  • Radio Operator License: Aeronautical Certified

With a strong foundation in aviation and an impressive track record of operational excellence, Suresh has successfully led countless drone missions across diverse applications. His deep technical knowledge and practical experience make him a trusted leader in the field.

In addition to his fieldwork, Suresh has a growing passion for education. Over the past few years, he has been actively involved in training aspiring drone pilots, sharing his knowledge and ensuring the next generation of UAV operators are safe, skilled, and confident.

Whether piloting complex drone operations or leading a classroom, Suresh brings precision, professionalism, and a deep commitment to excellence in everything he does.
